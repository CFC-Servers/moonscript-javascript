MoonScript Online Compiler (0.2.0-dev)


      

    


    

      

        

          
          
        

        
        
      

	  

		  The MoonScript online compiler runs in your browser through a version
		  of Lua with LPeg that has
		  been compiled to JavaScript using Emscripten. It's very slow on
		  Chrome, and reasonable on Firefox and Opera.  Find the source on github.
	  


	  

		  Find out more about MoonScript at
		  moonscript.org.
		  

		  Contact and updates on Twitter: @moonscript
